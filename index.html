A cool guide for beginners

1. The beginning of the game. At the start of the game, you only have a stone and a torch. With the help of a stone, you can extract various resources (wood, stone, metal), and with the help of a torch — to illuminate the darkness and explore the territory. 2. Resource extraction. With the help of an axe, you can mine wood and stone, as well as collect resources from various objects on the map. 3. The first weapon. To create the first weapon, you will need fabric and wood. 4. Creating a database. Start by finding a suitable location for the base. Then gather the necessary resources to build the base (walls, doors, windows, etc.) and start building. 5. Learning in the game. There are many types of weapons in the game, ranging from stone knives to more sophisticated weapons such as automatic rifles and rocket launchers. 6. Raids. In order to secure your base and preserve the extracted resources, you need to strengthen your defensive positions. It is also worth exploring explosives and raid tactics to successfully attack other players' bases and gain additional resources for your survival.
